Hello. I am Xinwei (pronounced as "shin way", 庄新伟), a 3rd year PhD student in Architecture. I am advised by Professor Luisa Caldas in the Building Science, Technology and Sustainability program from the College of Environmental Design (CED) at UC Berkeley, and I'm a member at Center of the Built Environment (CBE). My doctoral research interests lie in energy efficiency, sustainability, data driven design and evaluation, and mixed reality. I joined XR lab in 2020, where I helped developing the Virtual Bauer Wurster (VBW) project and assisted in a graduate level course taught by Prof. Caldas, the Architecture of Light: An Analytical Journey, where students use the VBW as a virtual space to narrate their design work. In summer 2022, I worked as a student research assistant for the Building Technology & Urban Systems Division at Berkeley Lawrence National Laboratory conducting data analysis for Zero-energy house.

  I'm looking for undergrad/graduate students to assist my current researches on machine learning and 3D reconstruction. Basic programming skills needed. Please email me if interested!
