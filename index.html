Hello. I am Xinwei (pronounced as "shin way", 庄新伟), a 3rd year PhD student in Architecture. I am advised by Professor Luisa Caldas, coadvised by Professor Simon Schleicher in the Building Science, Technology and Sustainability program from the College of Environmental Design (CED) at UC Berkeley. I'm a member of XR lab and Center of the Built Environment (CBE), and President at Immersive Design Student Club. My doctoral research interests lie in generative architecture and urban design, energy efficiency, sustainability, and mixed reality.
